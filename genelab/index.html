What is it?

jDev GeneLab, also known as Project HEGADL (Human External Gene Aggregation Database and Laboratory) is a project that aims to create human gene profiles only using externally-visible genes. These gene profiles will be aggregated and saved in the HEGADL public database and will be experimented with by the HEGADL virtual laboratory.

How does it work?

jDev GeneLab will need you to indicate some values (full name, country, height, biological gender, age, etc.) and it will then ask you to provide us with some facial information.

Privacy

We take privacy very seriously, we do not want to track you, we don't care about who you are, we care about what you are. In fact, we don't even store your name on our database, only your Subject Number is public. We don't want money or personal information, we want the whole picture, the aggregation of all of these external genes.
